About Me

Hi! I'm Elijah Cobb Game developer and game designer

I'm most comfortable when I get to fill the gaps in a game development team, from any angle that I'm needed. Level and game design is my foundation in work and in education at Northeastern University, but from there I've learned and become a confident programmer, 3D artist (I always look for excuses to use Substance Designer), narrative designer, and game producer. I love the process of making games from pre-production to polishing and everything between, and that drives me to do the best work wherever I can. This portfolio focuses on the projects I'm most proud of where I had a role in design or development.

You can find me at Boston area game jams, and making observations about the Olympics online.
